    What is Titans League?

    T90 Titans League (TTL) is a reoccurring Age of Empires 2 tournament, divided into 3 leagues:
    Platinum, Gold, and Silver.

    In Platinum, players are placed into 4 groups of 6. Each player will play a round of 3 games against the other players in their group. 5th and 6th seeds are relegated to Gold league for the following season. The 4th seed in each group does not advance, but is guaranteed a spot in Platinum the following season. The top 3 players advance to the playoffs bracket.

    Gold league follows a similar format, with 4 groups of 6. Each group's top players qualify for a decider match to determine who will advance to Platinum. The bottom 2 from each group are relegated to Silver league.

    Silver league also sees players placed in groups, however, players instead compete in a single-elimination "best-of" bracket. The winner of each group qualifies for the Gold leage in the following season.
